Welcome to Youth Leadership's very own website!

Program Info
UTOWN@UBC Youth Leadership Program is a program dedicated to youth practicing their leadership skills and making friends.

Activities and Events
Every Friday, the program offers youth different kinds of activities to enjoy, and allows youth to also organize events in the community.

Members and Leaders
Leadership's group members are each individually talented and amazing youth. Their leader, Taylor Scott, would like to introduce them to you more.

Outdoor Adventure Club
What does Leadership do in the summer? Meet Outdoor Adventure Club, a program that welcomes all youth to join and hike beautiful mountains!

Meeting place
First opened in 2016, the Wesbrook Community Centre embraces all community members, especially youth. This is where leadership meets every Friday, from 4pm to 7:30pm.
